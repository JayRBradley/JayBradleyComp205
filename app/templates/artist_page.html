Quail

Quail is ready to make you dance the night away. Based in Ithaca, New York, the eight piece band combines elements of Pop, R&B, Funk, and Rock styles in their music, which features a grooving rhythm section, fiery horn section, power vocals, and boogie-worthy arrangements.

Formed in November of 2018, Quail has gained a following around the Northeast. The Quail Trail gains more members everyday.

On November 14th 2019, Quail released their first single, "Blue Sky". The band is currently working on recording a full length studio album.
